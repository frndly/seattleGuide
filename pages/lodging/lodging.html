Places to Stay in Seattle

Lodging Types
  • Hotels and Motels
  • Vacation Rentals
  • Bed and Breakfasts
  • Hostels

From 5-star hotels to dormitory style hostels, the city of Seattle has a room for every type of tourist or local alike. Select one of the following options to create your ideal Seattle Stay.



Hotels and Motels

The City of Seattle has several different Hotel/Motels to choose from. There are 5-star hotels as well as more budget friendly options.

Belltown Inn

Every room has a kitchenette helping travelers save money on eating out.

Click to be directed to the Belltown Inn website.

Four Seasons Hotel Seattle

This five-star hotel, located in downtown Seattle, offers onsite dining and spa services. For those traveling with young children babysitting services are available as well.

Click to be directed to the Four Seasons Hotel Seattle website.

Hilton Seattle

Ideal location connected to the Washington State Convention Center with an on-site rental car company, enjoy the city views and easy access to shopping.

Click to be directed to the Hilton Seattle website.

The Inn at Queen Ann

Perfect for the traveler on a budget, located across the street from the Seattle Center, this quaint inn offers the convenience of a full-service hotel.

Click to be directed to The Inn at Queen Ann website.

Inn at the Market

Located in Pike Place Market, just steps away from the Seattle Waterfront, this first-class hotel is a real treasure.

Click to be directed to the Inn at the Market website.

The Paramount Hotel

A luxury hotel located within walking distance to many of Seattle’s favorite attractions.

Click to be directed to The Paramount Hotel website.

Bed and Breakfasts

Looking for a more private accommodation with the conveniences of staying at a hotel? Try out one of Seattle’s Bed and Breakfast locations.

Bacon Mansion

This 1909, structure is filled with history inside and out yet maintains modern day conveniences. Located on Capitol Hill.

Click to be directed to the Bacon Mansion website.

Ballard Inn

Built in 1902, but restored to provide their guests with modern amenities, offering rooms with private or shared baths.

Click to be directed to the Ballard Inn website.

Chelsea Station Inn

This is more than your traditional Bed and Breakfast, each room is a 900 square foot suite with everything from a double walk-in rain shower to black-out curtains, and a 24/7 complimentary pantry.

Click to be directed to the Chelsea Station Inn website.

Greenlake Guest House

Stay in this Craftsman house, with private bathrooms, across the street from Greenlake. This Bed and Breakfast now offers a complimentary wine and beer hour Monday through Friday.

Click to be directed to the Greenlake Guest House website.

Hostels

Traveling on a budget and desire more of the communal feel? Perhaps a Seattle Hostel is more your style.

Green Tortoise Hostel

Located across the street from Pike Place, with something for every traveler; bike rentals, computers, a self-catered kitchen, dorm rooms, private rooms, and family rooms.

Click to be directed to the Green Tortoise Hostel website.

Hostelling International Seattle at American Hotel

With its location in the International district public transportation is easily accessible.

Click to be directed to the Hostelling International Seattle at American Hotel website.
